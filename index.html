About Hack In The North

Hack In The North is a collaboration of programmers and designers to code, design and build innovative solutions to transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge based economy. It provides a venue for self-expression and creativity through technology.

Hack In The North 1.0 was successfully held from April 1-3, 2016 at IIIT Allahabad in collaboration with Mozilla and HackerEarth. It saw amazing participation of 200+ participants from across the country.

And, It was Grand!!

After 36 hours of intense hacking, the hackers came up with innovative projects solving some real life problems.

Now, It's time for HITN 2.0!!

Bigger.Better. That's the word. 300+ Hackers this time. Largest student Hackathon in India.

Undergraduates, graduates and everyone interested in tech are welcome. It doesn't matter if you are still making your first website, there are a lot of things to learn. Students from IITs, NITs, IIITs and other renowned institutes will form the major portion of the participants like Hack In The North 1.0.

The only constraint is time. You get only 36 hours to give your best.

FAQ

What is HackInTheNorth?

HackInTheNorth is the annual Hackathon organized at Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad. It is one of the largest student-organized hackathons in India.

Who runs the HackInTheNorth?

HackInTheNorth is run by Aparoksha Team and GeekHaven(Technical Society of IIIT Allahabad).

Can we apply as a team?

Not quite -- you and your intended teammates should all submit individual applications. But if you get an invitation and some of your teammates don't, shoot us an email at geekhaven@iiita.ac.in and we'll try to sort things out.

How and when will I hear back?

We'll be releasing decisions weekly - if you've submitted your application by a Friday, you'll be getting an email back by the next Friday!

Who can apply?

If you are enrolled in a high school, college, or university, or if you left school during the current school year, you are good to go!!

What should I bring?

We'll send you a comprehensive list once it's closer to showtime, but it'll be basically what you'd need for any sleepover: laptop, charger, deodorant (!), change of clothes. You won't need food or drinks; we've got you covered there.

What do people normally make?

You can make anything you like. Most of the hacks revolve around web or phone apps, or working with hardware.

What are you looking for in an application?

Above anything else -- passion . Tell us what you care about, what excites you. We want to know what'll drive you to start and continue hacking, whether it's your first time or your fifteenth. If there's something about you that stands out from the "typical" hacker, we'd love to hear about that, too!

Will you be offering travel reimbursements?

We would support you if you can't afford to travel to HackInTheNorth. Shoot us a mail if you need reimbursements.

How much does it cost to attend?

Zero. HackInTheNorth is free for everyone.
